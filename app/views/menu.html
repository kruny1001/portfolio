Kevin S. Portfolio

Kevin S. is an professional software engineer at
Department of Mathematics and Statistics, South Dakota State University. He currently develops and conducts research in the general area of bioinformatics and e-Learning platform for statistics subject.

Currently, the main focus of his project is the role of stablizing the platform and take charge of end-user satisfaction. Another emphasis is the development of tools and resources for the interpretation of high-throughput genomics data. Kevin S. also enjoys working with many collaborators in a supporting role and open source group.

His position is supported by National Science Foundation (NSF), National Institutes of Health (NIH), state of South Dakota, Sanford Health etc.
